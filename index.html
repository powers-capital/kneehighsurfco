Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you offer Free Shipping?
    Yes! We primarily sell our suits on Amazon where they often come with Free 2-Day Shipping
  • Can I return the wetsuit?
    Yes! We have a 100% satisfaction guarantee... whatever you don't like, just send it back!
  • What size should I buy for my 6 month old?
    Our X-Small (0T) wetsuit fits babies from 6 months up to 1.5 years.  Remember all babies are different sizes but this is a good generalization.
  • How tight should the suit fit?                         
    Our suits are meant to be snug to provide the most warmth.  The suit should never cut off circulation or heavily restrict movement. For ease of getting your child in the suit, ensure the suit and your child are dry and it should go on smoothly with a little bit of effort.
  • Do you have larger sizes?
    Currently we only have our 0T and 2T in stock.  We are hoping to have our 4T, 6T, and 8T suits available by winter of 2022. Join our mailing list to get notified or to inquire about beta product testing.
